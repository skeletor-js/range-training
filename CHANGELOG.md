## [0.1.0] - 2026-01-06

### Added

- Initial public release
- Session tracking with date, location, duration, and weather
- Precision shot marking with metrics (extreme spread, mean radius, MOA, group center offset)
- Firearm inventory with round count tracking
- Ammunition inventory with caliber, brand, grain weight, and bullet type
- Ammunition purchase logging with price tracking
- Firearm-ammo compatibility ratings
- 25 built-in standardized drills
- Custom drill creation
- Drill attempt tracking with multiple scoring types
- Personal best tracking and performance trends
- Shooting goals with optional target dates
- Activity heatmap visualization
- PWA support with offline functionality
- Database export/import (SQLite and JSON formats)
- Dark mode support
