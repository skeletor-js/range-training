# Phase 2: Inventory Enhancement

## Objective
Turn basic equipment tracking into a rich inventory management system with purchase history, price trends, and performance reviews.

## Component Parts / Feature Branches

### 1. Purchase History (`feat/ammo-purchases`)
- Log ammo purchases (Quantity, Price, Seller, Date).
- Display Purchase History per ammo type.
- Calculate and show "Price per Round" (PPR) metrics and benchmarks.

### 2. Ammo Reviews & Ratings (`feat/ammo-reviews`)
- Star rating system for ammo types.
- Specific ratings for Reliability and Accuracy.
- Free-form review notes.

### 3. Compatibility Tracking (`feat/firearm-compatibility`)
- Link specific ammo types to specific firearms with "Load Notes."
- Track which firearms perform best (or worst) with specific brand/grain combinations.

## Definition of Done
- User can see a history of what they paid for a specific caliber over time.
- Ammo list shows star ratings and reliability notes at a glance.
